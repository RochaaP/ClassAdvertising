mtute.lk

Mtute.lk was founded on a vision to build an online institution that is better and more affordable than traditional options.

Our mission is to provide a better distance learning platform for island wide student community while helping them to choose best instructors and institutes in Sri Lanka. It will lead to solve the educational difference problem by giving students the opportunity to get quality education equally.
